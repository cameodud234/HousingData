This dataset contains records of buildings/building units sold in New York City on the property market over a year. The data is sourced from the New York City Department of Financeâ€™s Rolling Sales dataset from September 2017. There are 22 attributes and 84,548 rows within the dataset.

1 Label Encoder, 2 Ordinal Features, 19 OneHot Encoded Features obtained an accuracy of 14.48% on 10% of the test data and an accuracy of 13.19% on 33% test data.

Classification was performed on sales price using naive bayes and an id3 decision tree.
